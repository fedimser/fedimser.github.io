Tetris

Classic "Tetris" game.
You have to direct falling tetraminos in order to form solid layers. If that happens, the layer will disappear.
More layers - more score. More score - higher level. Higher level - faster falling tetraminos.
If tetraminos have no space to fall, game is over.
There are 5 types of tetramino in the game.
You may change colors and size of field.
Controls: mouse/ keyboard arrows.
Language: Russian.

Download
.NET Framework 4 is required.
