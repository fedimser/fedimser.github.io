Gallery of fractals

All fractals below were rendered with program written by me. They all are images of sets on complex plane. For each pixel some corresponding complex number was taken and some particular series, beginning with that number was calculated. On bicolor images color represents, if series converges or not. On monochrome images color, besides convergence, represents its speed.
