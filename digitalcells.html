Digital Cells is a logic Flash-game similar to the barley-break game, but hexagonal.

Game field is hexagonal-shaped and consists of tokens with numbers, located in nodes of hexagonal grid.
During one move you can rotate 6 tokens around one token (unless it is on the edge of the field). In order to do that you should click on central token. If you click on the left part, tokens rotate counterclockwise. If you click on the right part, tokens rotate clockwise.
The goal of the game is to arrange tokens by ascending of numbers.
There are three levels available: with 19, 37 and 61 tokens.
